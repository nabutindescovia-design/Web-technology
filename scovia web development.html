General Information about UMU Inter-Campus Competitions

The Uganda Martyrs University (UMU) Inter-Campus Competitions are annual sports and cultural events that bring together students from all UMU campuse
— including Nkozi, Masaka, Rubaga, Mbale, and Kabale. The competitions are organized to promote unity, teamwork, and talent development among students.

    Main Games Played The competitions feature a variety of sports and games such as:
  1. Football – The most popular event where campuses compete for the UMU Football Trophy.
  2. Netball – A major sport among female students promoting teamwork and coordination.
  3. Volleyball – Played by both male and female teams to enhance fitness and spirit.
  4. Athletics – Including short races, relays, and long-distance events.
